Academics in the Wild

AITW is an initiative to support physicists and mathematicians in their journey from academia to industry.

The initiative started as a podcast called "Physicists in the Wild", where we interviewed physicists who turned their PhD training into diverse and often unconventional careers.

We are now building a free community, to be centered around the AITW Discord server, where we will regularly host informative and supportive events related to academia-industry career transitions.
